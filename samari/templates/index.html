About Us

Some tidbits about us

Arianna Victoria Smith Bride

Arianna was born to Tim and Lisa Smith on June 2, 1997. She grew up in Bloomsburg, Pennsylvania and attended Calvary Baptist Church, where her father serves as the senior pastor. She currently is a senior English major at Bob Jones University and will be graduating with her Bachelor's degree in December. Some of her favorite things are coffee, books, fuzzy blankets, cheesy Hallmark movies, and deep conversations.

Samuel Edward Henry Groom

Samuel was born to Andrew and Heather Henry on June 4, 1997 in Jacksonville, Florida. He grew up in McDonough, Georgia and attended Bible Baptist Church. Sam graduated in May 2019 with his Bachelor's in Computer Science. He currently works as a software developer for Follett School Solutions, a company based in Illinois that creates software for schools. Some of his favorite things are running, playing piano, programming, and nerdy or good talks with friends.

I can't brag about my love for God because I fail Him daily, but I can brag about His love for me because it never fails.

There is never a time or place for true love. It happens accidentally, in a heartbeat, in a single flashing, throbbing moment.

Love lets you find those hidden places in another person, even the ones they didn’t know were there, even the ones they wouldn’t have thought to call beautiful themselves.

Our Love Story

We're in love!

27
aug
2017

First met

We met at the beginning of Arianna's Sophomore year and Samuel's Junior year of college. God had separately led us both to begin attending and serving at Grace Baptist Fellowship. We carpooled to church with two of our closest friends, and Arianna immediately noticed Samuel’s kind and friendly nature. She began running into him everywhere after that and was afraid he thought she was stalking him. But they quickly realized how similiar their backgrounds were and developed a fast friendship.

19
oct
2017

First Date

Our first "official" date was Artist Series at Bob Jones University. We went with the same people that we carpooled with when we first met. It was an absolute blast! Arianna was blown away by how Sam asked her to this event, by spelling out “Artist Series?” in tea lights on top of a pavilion back campus at BJU. How romantic!

7
jan
2018

Started Dating

Samuel attended a wedding of a good friend in Maryland and traveled up to see Arianna the day after. On that day, we began dating. Arianna’s brother Josiah had given Sam some requirements before he could date Arianna. One of those requirements was making paper cranes, so when Sam asked Arianna he made sure to include some paper cranes. Dating has been a whirlwind, joy, and blessing ever since!

22
mar
2018

I Love You

We went to New York City during spring break. After a wonderful, full day of activity with friends, we went to the top of the Empire State Building. It was a clear night with a beautiful view of the New York skyline, and we shared our first “I love you!”

17
may
2019

Engaged!

We went on a missions trip to Antigua in May of 2019. On the last day there, the missionaries and team leaders helped Samuel set up for the engagement. We got engaged on one of the most beautiful beaches on the island! Arianna was completely surprised by the proposal. Samuel had arranged a flower heart on the beach for Arianna and before proposing sang a song he had written for her. And we were able to share half an hour on the beach celebrating our engagement by ourselves. It was absolutely perfect!
